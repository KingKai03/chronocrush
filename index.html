How to Play

CHRONOCRUSH: The Era Odyssey

1

Tap to Select

Tap any tile on the grid to select it — it glows gold. Then tap an adjacent tile (up, down, left or right) to swap them.

2

Match 3 in a Row

Line up 3 or more identical tiles in a row or column. They flash gold and vanish — new tiles drop in to replace them!

3

Beat the Challenge

Each level has a tile challenge (e.g. "Clear 9 📻 Radios") AND a score target. Hit both before your 20 moves run out to win!

4

Earn Stars & Gold

Finish a level to earn 1–3 📀 stars based on your score. Stars earn you 🪙 gold coins to spend in the shop.

5

Use Boosters

Stuck? Use your boosters:
🔨 Hammer — smashes any single tile
💣 Time Bomb — clears a 3×3 area
🔀 Shuffle — reshuffles the whole board

6

Conquer the Eras

There are 9 musical eras — from 1940s Noir to the 2002 Flip Phone Era — each with 10 levels and unique tiles. Complete an era to earn a 🥇 Gold, 🥈 Silver, or 🥉 Bronze Trophy!

7

Watch your Lives ❤️

You start with 5 lives. Running out of moves without completing a level costs 1 life. Refill lives in the 🛒 Shop using gold coins.
