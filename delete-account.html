🗑️ Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 2025

You can permanently delete your CHRONOCRUSH account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion and what data is removed.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your game progress, gold coins, trophies, and account information will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

Account deletion is done directly inside the CHRONOCRUSH app in a few simple steps:

  1. Open the CHRONOCRUSH app on your device
  2. From the main map screen, tap the ⚙️ Settings icon in the top-right corner
  3. Scroll down and tap "🗑️ Deactivate Account"
  4. Read the warning carefully — it lists everything that will be deleted
  5. Tick the confirmation checkbox: "I understand this is permanent and cannot be undone"
  6. Tap "Delete My Account" — you may be asked to re-authenticate with Google for security
  7. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately

What Data Is Deleted

Data Type Action Timeframe
Google account sign-in Permanently deleted Immediately
Display name & email address Permanently deleted Immediately
Game progress & level records Permanently deleted Immediately
Gold coins & booster inventory Permanently deleted Immediately
Daily reward streak Permanently deleted Immediately
Trophy & awards records Permanently deleted Immediately
Device preferences (sound/vibrate) Permanently deleted Immediately
Purchase transaction records Retained for legal/tax compliance Up to 7 years (legal requirement)

Guest Players

If you played as a Guest (no Google Sign-In), all your data is stored locally on your device only. To delete it simply:

  • Clear your browser/app data in your device settings, or
  • Open Settings in the game → tap "Reset Progress" to wipe all local data

Guest players have no account on our servers — there is nothing to delete remotely.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, or if you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us and we will manually process your deletion request within 30 days.
