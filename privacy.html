Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

CHRONOCRUSH: The Era Odyssey ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

If you play as a Guest: We collect no personal information. All game progress is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers.

If you sign in with Google: We receive from Google your display name, email address, and profile photo URL. This information is used solely to identify your account and personalise your experience.

Game data: We store your game progress, gold balance, level records, booster inventory, and daily streak information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: (a) maintain and identify your game account; (b) save and sync your game progress; (c) process in-app purchases; (d) send notifications you have opted into; (e) improve the Game based on usage patterns.

4. Data Storage

Guest player data is stored exclusively on your device using browser localStorage. Signed-in player data may be stored on secure Firebase servers operated by Google LLC. Firebase's data centres comply with international security standards.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Firebase (Google LLC) — authentication and data storage; Google Sign-In — account authentication. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. Your information is only shared with service providers necessary to operate the Game.

7. Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, we may send you notifications about daily challenges, rewards, and game updates. You can withdraw this permission at any time through your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: (a) access the personal data we hold about you; (b) correct inaccurate data; (c) request deletion of your data; (d) withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, use the "Deactivate Account" option in Settings, which will permanently delete all your data.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deactivation, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

11. Cookies and Local Storage

The Game uses browser localStorage to save your preferences and game progress locally on your device. This is essential for the Game to function.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the in-game Announcements page.

13. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the Game's official channels or at privacy@chronocrush.game.
